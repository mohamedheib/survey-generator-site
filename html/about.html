About
  • TGS (test generator site) is a website that comes to assist users who want to create a basic survey/test and share it with their friends, coworkers, and any other users.

  • By using SGS you can create your own test and basically share this test unique id with your friends who can do this test and getting a score completely without log-in or sing-in into SGS.

  • The main idea of this site that anyone can create a survey/test and share it in a completely anonymous way and that can help people that want to add their employer interviews questions and afraid to get fired :), or people who want to share their knowledge on any industry without dealing with all the websites bureaucracy.
